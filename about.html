Biography

They banded together with a shared passion for alternative rock, and first met at an organization known as School of Rock, in 2017, which teaches children and teens how to train and perform as a band. The four members, Andrew Jagos, Riley Kane, Colby Brookshire, and Sarah Suarez were chosen to perform a show together and they immediately bonded. Unable to ignore their chemistry with music and friendship, they decided to officially form their own band: The ARCS PROJECT. The name was derived from the first letter of each members’ names, giving the catchy word, ARCS. Later added to the group, Abby Jagos (Andrew’s sister and a keyboardist), met with the band at another show hosted by the School of Rock and they have been performing together ever since.
